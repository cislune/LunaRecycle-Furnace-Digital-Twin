Part 5: Carbon Deficit

Carbon in carbothermal reduction is required both to remove oxygen from metal oxides (via CO/CO₂ formation), and to satisfy equilibrium carbon partitioning into metal and slag phases. FactSage assumes this carbon inventory is supplied and therefore cannot determine whether it is achievable from the pyrolysis process. This gate compares available process carbon to the equilibrium-implied requirement, indicating nominal or carbon-limited operation.
